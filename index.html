Mea Culpa Hearts

Swap sword attributes: Heart of Oils fire dmg, Heart of the Holy Purge fervor gain. Find them across Cvstodia to adapt your playstyle.

Fervor System

Fervor fuels divine prayers. Upgrade Bile Flasks at Nacimiento statues and increase fervor capacity with rosary beads like Quicksilver.

The Three Endings & Ascension Mode

Ending A (Believer)

Defeat Escribar without completing Holy Wounds. The Penitent One succumbs to the throne, endless cycle persists.

Ending B (Good)

Base game completion with all three wounds. The Miracle weakens but hope lingers.

Ending C (True Ending)

Wounds of Eventide DLC: Confess all statues, equip Apodictic Heart, defeat Crisanta and face the High Wills. The Miracle is shattered — ultimate liberation.

Ascension Mode (NG+) — Unlock after any ending. Choose Penitence: Unwavering Faith (no flask recovery, fervor spells), True Guilt (tears drop on death), Bleeding Heart (health orbs). New bosses and rewards await.
