Lore & The Miracle

Setting: Cvstodia

A cursed land gripped by the Miracle — a divine but twisted force that manifests guilt into grotesque flesh and eternal penance. The Penitent One, silent and masked, breaks the cycle of the Grievous Miracle.

The Penitent One

Last survivor of the Brotherhood of the Silent Sorrow. Wielding Mea Culpa, a sword born from guilt, he journeys to halt the perpetual torment and face the High Wills.

Key Factions

  • Anointed Legion – corrupted soldiers.
  • Confessor & Visages – manifestations of guilt.
  • The Sorrowful – twisted by miracle's grief.

Combat & Mea Culpa Heart

Mea Culpa Abilities

  • Combo – 4-hit slash, heavy finisher.
  • Dash Strike – sliding attack, breaks enemy poise.
  • Verdiales of the Forsaken Hamlet – ranged projectile (prayer).
  • Mea Culpa Hearts – alter sword mechanics (e.g., Heart of Oils, increased fervor generation).

Parry & Executions

Precise blocking deflects attacks and opens enemies for executions. Executions grant invincibility frames and extra Tears of Atonement. Mastering parry is key for early bosses.

Fervor & Bile Flasks

Fervor fuels prayers (special moves). Increase flask count via Nacimiento statues. Upgrades also enhance healing potency.
Tip Explore Albero for first upgrades.

Knots & Altarpieces

Altarpieces (Wounds of Eventide) grant powerful synergies: equip two matching figures for unique effects like increased parry window or life leech.

Key Quest Items

Holy Wounds (three): Wound of Abnegation, Contrition, Compunction — used to access the true ending path. Immaculate Bead and Custodia of the Sin unlock final boss variation. Collect all before fighting Crisanta for "Ending C".

Endings & New Game+ (A to Z)

Ending A: The Path of the Believer

Defeat Escribar without completing the Holy Wounds quest. The Penitent One takes the throne, continuing the endless cycle of penance.

Ending B: The Good Ending

Complete the Holy Wounds (3 wounds) and defeat Crisanta without the immaculate bead? Actually true ending (Ending C) requires extra steps. In original base, Ending B breaks the cycle partially.

Ending C (True Ending - Wounds of Eventide)

Complete all: confessor statues, give all Holy Wounds, defeat Crisanta after equipping the Apodictic Heart, then face Escribar and finally fight the High Wills. Penitent One destroys the Miracle's root. Most canon ending.

Ascension Mode (NG+): Unlocks after any ending. Adds new boss challenges, Penitence systems (Unwavering Faith, True Guilt, Bleeding Heart) that modify gameplay drastically.

Secret Areas & Tips

Hidden Where Olive Trees Wither: Use blood platforms to reach secret boss Isidora, Voice of the Dead. Pro Tip Use "Miriam's Challenges" (Stir of Dawn DLC) for new prayers and skins. For maximum penitence, always equip Heart of the Virtuous Pain to boost Tears gain.
